Pork with Dumplings and Cabbage

Description

In Chinese culture, we make dumplings as a family to bring good luck and fortune for the Lunar New Year!
It's a wonderful cooking activity to do together with loved ones. It's also a very popular Chinese food that you can find at many Chinese restaurants for dim sum.

ingredients

  • Regular soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Cornstarch
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Chop cabbage finely into small pieces.
  2. Transfer cabbage to a large bowl. Add salt and massage into leaves.
  3. Let it sit for 10 minutes to drain excess water.
  4. Rinse cabbage of salt with cold water.
  5. Then transfer cabbage to a clean towel and squeeze out excess water as best you can.
  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground pork with filling ingredients along with drained cabbage as listed above.
  7. Mix in one direction until the filling becomes a paste. The meat should be broken down and sticky, about 4 minutes.
  8. Optional tip: take a 1 tablespoon of the filling and pan fry or microwave for 60 seconds until cooked. Taste the filling and if needed, adjust the raw filling by adding more seasoning as you desire.
